Your Ads Privacy Choices

At ShopSphere, we value your privacy. This page explains how we use personalized advertising and provides you with options to manage your ad preferences.

1. Why We Use Personalized Ads

Personalized ads allow us to show you more relevant products and offers based on your browsing behavior, preferences, and interactions with our site.

2. What Data Do We Use?

To provide personalized ads, we may collect and use the following types of information:

  • Your browsing history on ShopSphere
  • Interactions with ads and promotions
  • Device and location data (if allowed)
  • Demographic and interest information (provided voluntarily or inferred)

3. Your Privacy Choices

You have control over the ads you see. Here are your options:

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt-out of receiving personalized ads by clicking the button below.
  • Adjust Browser Settings: Modify your browser’s cookie settings to block tracking cookies.
  • Use Ad Preferences Tools: Platforms like Google and Facebook provide tools to manage ad preferences.

4. Third-Party Advertisers

We may partner with third-party advertisers to deliver ads. These advertisers may use their own cookies and tracking technologies. For more details, please review their privacy policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about our ad practices or privacy choices, please contact us.
